The Vienna Underground is part of Vienna's public transport system and carries over one million people per day.
It went into trial operation in 1976 and was finally officially opened in 1978.
The underground network currently consists of five lines. The underground is operated by Verkehrsgesellschaft Wiener Linien GmbH & Co KG, which belongs to Wiener Stadtwerke and is owned by the City of Vienna.

On this website we would like to provide you with more information of Vienna's underground trains in order to make your journey as pleasant as possible!
